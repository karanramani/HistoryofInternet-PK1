The Invention of the Internet

Evolution of internet has bought tremendous changes over the centuries with the form of communication and brining people from different parts of the world together. People of all the ages have learnt the value of technology including its pros and cons. Computer has helped people connect together over the years and has made distance disappear. Operating system runs on the phone, computer, IPad, TV…etc.…this is a huge shift in the era.

Internet helps keep us update with things happening everywhere around the world. First the computers where humongous super large and a lot of labor was require to cool those computers as too much heat could break down those enormous computers. Maintenance played a significant role during that time. Then slowly sizes of computers and microprocessors started to get minute and this improvised the maintenance factor. This resolved a lot of heating issue as fan was introduced within the PCs then helped computer run smoothly.

Speed of network plays an important role. First when the internet was developed the wait time was most then slowly changes emerged and then from there onwards within the matter of seconds emails were sent and received. So many web based applications have now come into play. People use applications such as emails, Facebook, Instagram, etc. to communicate with people. We are slowly moving from IPv4 to IPv6 network as the internet traffic is increasing day by day. People are becoming tech savvy and have bought the world to net.

Security plays a key role as privacy is must this is the key focus in this era. Technology communication plays a key role in Government, military, navy, and air-force. ARPANET came in then into defense network for tight security so no one unleash their messages. As technology kept on expanding security got updated on daily basis. In 1992, we had only 50 webpages but now we have lost the count of the number of webpages we have on our end. Advertising is done through technology. Internet has changed the world and the value of communication.
